Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in Time Travel (the "App"), developed by Peter Zhang.

Information Collection and Use

Apple Music Integration

Our App integrates with Apple Music via MusicKit to provide you with historical music playback and related features. When you authorize the App to access Apple Music, the App accesses your Apple Music subscription status and playback capabilities. We do not store, collect, or transmit your Apple Music account details or listening history to any external servers.

Microphone Access

The App requests access to your microphone to support specific audio features. The microphone is used strictly for local processing on your device. We do not record, collect, or transmit any audio data to our servers or third parties.

Network Access

The App requires an active internet connection to fetch public music catalog data, historical album information, and to stream audio from Apple Music. No personally identifiable information is transmitted during these processes.

Data Storage

Any data related to your preferences or app state is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain any user accounts or external databases containing your personal information.

Third-Party Services

The App utilizes Apple's native services (such as MusicKit). Your use of these services is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not share any of your data with advertising networks or data brokers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us through the App Store contact details or by opening an issue on our GitHub repository.
